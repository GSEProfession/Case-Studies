Defra Noise Modelling System

Stage in GSE Lifecyle

Primary stage : Create

Related stage(s) : Define/Design

Defra is building a digital noise modelling system to map exposure to noise across England.

The model will be capable of:

  • Producing noise exposure maps to meet existing obligations for mapping road, rail and some industrial noise under the Environmental Noise (England) Regulations 2006
  • Defra is building an innovative digital twin to map exposure to noise across England
  • Producing noise exposure data for the Public Health Outcomes Framework and the 25 Year Environment Plan
  • Uploading additional datasets – e.g. areas of greater detail; multiple pollutants
  • Scenario planning and evidence development

BODY

  • Defra & other government departments/agencies
  • Designated public sector users (local authorities; highways/railway authorities)
  • The general public, who will be able to view the results
